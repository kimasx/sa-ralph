Stella Artois came to Ralph to run a programme to reward outlets that serve Stella Artois correctly.

The Connoisseurs Program

To further promote Stella Artois as the premium lager of choice and energise the on-trade about Stella Artois, we launched the Stella Artois Connoisseurs programme; a mammoth on-trade rewards, loyalty and training scheme, designed to encourage and recognise venues to consistently present and pour the perfect Stella Artois.
